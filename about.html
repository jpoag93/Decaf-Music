Welcome to my Website

I first created my music publishing company, Decaf Music, back in 1987. Every song I’ve ever written has been filed with this entity and registered with Broadcast Music Inc. (BMI), the organization that tracks my tunes and pays my music royalties.

This site is an extension of that. It is a place where I will archive all my songs – audio content, lyrics, etc. – where any recording artist, famous or not yet famous, can listen to the songs and read about them. In the event someone wants to record something of mine, that artist can contact me and negotiate terms.

If folks just want to listen and/or read, they can do that too. And if they want to purchase music for their own consumption, there is a link for that.

You will find all kinds of songs here – happy and sad, serious and frivolous – and they cut across many genres – blues, folk, rockabilly, Americana, bluegrass, gospel, rhythm & blues. My forte is the story song.

As I get older and more selective about the gigs I play, I am shifting my focus from performing to publishing. The creation of this site is a first step toward that end. More important, it is a signal that I am taking both myself and my craft seriously these days.

So browse away. Drop me a line if you’re so inclined.

Enjoy,
